<p><strong>The Marine Bio Fuel Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Distillate Fuel Oil</li><li>Residual Fuel Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine biofuel oil market primarily consists of two types: distillate fuel oil and residual fuel oil. Distillate fuel oil, often referred to as marine diesel oil, is refined to meet stricter environmental standards and is used in newer vessels for cleaner combustion. Residual fuel oil, derived from the heavier fractions of crude oil, is used in older ships and larger vessels, offering a cost-effective solution but producing higher emissions. Both types are integral to the shipping industry's transition towards sustainable fuels.</p></p>
