<p><strong>The Jet Bridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Apron drive jet bridge</li><li>Nose-loader jet bridge</li><li>Dual jet bridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jet bridges are essential for seamless boarding and deplaning of passengers at airports. The apron drive jet bridge operates from the apron, allowing flexibility in positioning. Nose-loader jet bridges connect directly to the aircraft's nose, ideal for smaller aircraft. Dual jet bridges accommodate two aircraft simultaneously at one gate, enhancing efficiency in busy airports. Each type responds to varying operational needs, maximizing passenger flow and airport efficiency. Understanding these market types is crucial for effective airport infrastructure planning.</p></p>
