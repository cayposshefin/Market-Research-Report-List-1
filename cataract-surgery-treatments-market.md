<p><p>The cataract surgery treatments market comprises two primary types: traditional cataract surgery and laser-assisted cataract surgery. Traditional cataract surgery involves manually removing the clouded lens and replacing it with an artificial intraocular lens. In contrast, laser-assisted cataract surgery utilizes advanced femtosecond laser technology to improve precision in lens fragmentation and capsulotomy, potentially enhancing surgical outcomes and recovery. Both approaches are aimed at restoring vision impaired by cataracts, with varying techniques and technology levels to suit patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
