<p><p>The Automatic Identification System (AIS) market consists of two primary classes: Class A and Class B. Class A AIS is mandated for large vessels over 300 gross tons and certain passenger ships, providing real-time data on vessel identity, position, course, and speed to enhance maritime safety. Class B AIS is designed for smaller vessels and recreational boats, offering similar functionalities with less stringent requirements. Both classes improve situational awareness, collision avoidance, and effective maritime traffic management.</p></p>
