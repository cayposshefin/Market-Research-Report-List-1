<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Type</li><li>Transverse Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The finned tube air-cooled heat exchanger market comprises two primary types: longitudinal and transverse. Longitudinal types feature fins arranged parallel to the flow of air, enhancing heat transfer efficiency in applications where airflow direction is consistent, making them suitable for larger installations. Conversely, transverse types have fins positioned perpendicular to the air stream, optimizing performance in compact spaces with variable airflow patterns. Both designs cater to diverse industrial needs, including power generation, HVAC, and chemical processing, promoting energy efficiency and operational effectiveness.</p></p>
