<p><p>The semi-rigid foams market is divided into two main types based on elastic modulus: low elastic modulus and high elastic modulus. Low elastic modulus foams are flexible and exhibit higher compressibility, making them suitable for applications like packaging and cushioning. In contrast, high elastic modulus foams are denser and more rigid, offering greater structural support and durability, ideal for automotive and construction applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, balancing comfort and performance based on specific requirements.</p></p>
