<p><ul><li>Rail Side Axle Counter</li><li>On-rail Axle Counter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The axle counter market for train detection is divided into two main types: rail side axle counters and on-rail axle counters. Rail side axle counters are installed alongside the tracks and detect trains by monitoring axle movements as they pass a designated point. In contrast, on-rail axle counters are mounted directly on the trains or tracks, providing real-time data and improved accuracy. Both types enhance operational safety and efficiency in railway systems by ensuring effective train monitoring and management.</p></p>
