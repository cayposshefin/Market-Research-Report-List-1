<p><p>The teletherapy machines market includes several advanced technologies aimed at improving cancer treatment. Intensity-Modulated Radiation Therapy (IMRT) allows for precise dose distribution to tumors while sparing surrounding healthy tissue. Image-Guided Radiotherapy (IGRT) enhances accuracy by integrating imaging techniques for real-time tumor tracking. Stereotactic Technology focuses on delivering high doses of radiation to targeted areas with minimal exposure to adjacent tissues. Proton Beam Therapy uses protons instead of traditional X-rays, offering a distinct advantage in treating specific tumor types with reduced side effects.</p></p>
