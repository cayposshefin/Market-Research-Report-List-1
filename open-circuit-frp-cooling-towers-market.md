<p><p>Open-circuit FRP (Fiberglass Reinforced Plastic) cooling towers are categorized into two main types: free ventilation and mechanical ventilation. Free ventilation cooling towers utilize natural airflow to dissipate heat, relying on aerodynamic design and atmospheric conditions. In contrast, mechanical ventilation cooling towers employ fans to enhance airflow, ensuring consistent and efficient cooling regardless of environmental conditions. Both types are vital for industrial and commercial applications, offering durability and resistance to corrosion while optimizing thermal performance.</p></p>
