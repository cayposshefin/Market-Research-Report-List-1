<p><strong>The Well Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Openwell</li><li>Borewell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The well pumps market can be categorized into two primary types: open well and borewell pumps. Open well pumps are designed for shallow water extraction, typically used in agricultural and residential settings where groundwater is easily accessible. Borewell pumps, on the other hand, are meant for deeper wells and are used extensively in areas with limited surface water. Both types cater to diverse needs, with open well pumps being more affordable and borewell pumps offering higher efficiency for deeper water sources.</p></p>
