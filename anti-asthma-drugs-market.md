<p><p>The anti-asthma drugs market comprises various treatment types aimed at managing asthma symptoms. Bronchodilators relax airway muscles, providing immediate relief from bronchospasm. Leukotriene antagonists block substances that cause inflammation, reducing asthma attacks. Mast cell stabilizers prevent the release of inflammatory chemicals, offering preventive care. Corticosteroids reduce inflammation in the airways, effectively managing long-term symptoms. Monoclonal antibodies target specific pathways in the immune system, offering advanced therapy for severe asthma. These diverse options ensure tailored treatment for asthma patients.</p></p>
