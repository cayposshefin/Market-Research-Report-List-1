<p><p>The rotary selector switches market is categorized into two main types: single-pole and multi-pole rotary selector switches. Single-pole rotary switches control a single circuit, allowing users to select one of several options, making them ideal for simple applications. Multi-pole rotary switches, on the other hand, can control multiple circuits simultaneously, enabling more complex configurations in industrial and electronic devices. Both types are widely used in various sectors, including automotive, telecommunications, and consumer electronics, for efficient control and selection.</p></p>
