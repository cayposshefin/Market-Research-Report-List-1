<p><strong>The Immunohistochemistry（IHC） Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traditional Direct Method</li><li>New Direct Method</li><li>Enzyme Bridgemethod</li><li>PAP Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Immunohistochemistry (IHC) testing involves various methodologies for detecting specific antigens in tissue samples. The Traditional Direct Method uses labeled antibodies to bind directly to antigens, while the New Direct Method incorporates optimized reagents for improved sensitivity. The Enzyme Bridge Method utilizes a secondary enzyme-labeled antibody to amplify the signal, enhancing detection. The PAP (Peroxidase-Antiperoxidase) Method employs a complex of peroxidase-labeled antibodies for visualization, offering high specificity and sensitivity. Each method offers unique advantages for diagnosing diseases.</p></p>
