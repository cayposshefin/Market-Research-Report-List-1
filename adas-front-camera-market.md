<p><strong>The ADAS Front Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monocular</li><li>Binocular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ADAS (Advanced Driver Assistance Systems) front camera market is divided into two primary types: monocular and binocular. Monocular cameras utilize a single lens to capture visual data, relying on algorithms to interpret depth and context, making them cost-effective and simpler to install. In contrast, binocular cameras feature two lenses, enabling true depth perception and improved accuracy in detecting objects and obstacles. This distinction influences their applications, with monocular systems suitable for basic features and binocular systems supporting advanced functionalities like 3D mapping.</p></p>
