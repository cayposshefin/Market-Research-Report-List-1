<p><p>The thermosetting composites market is categorized into three main types: Short Fiber Reinforced Thermosets (SFRT), Long Fiber Reinforced Thermosets (LFRT), and Glass Fiber Reinforced Thermosets (GFRT). SFRT utilizes short fibers to enhance mechanical properties while maintaining a low weight, making it suitable for various applications. LFRT employs longer fibers for improved strength and toughness, catering to demanding structural applications. GFRT incorporates glass fibers for excellent strength and thermal resistance, widely used in automotive, aerospace, and electrical sectors.</p></p>
