<p><p>The NR Latex Concentrates market is categorized into three main types based on ammonia content: high ammonia, low ammonia, and medium ammonia. High ammonia latex contains a higher percentage of ammonia, enhancing shelf-life but with potential odor issues, making it suitable for certain industrial applications. Low ammonia latex, with reduced ammonia levels, offers better odor control, ideal for products like gloves and medical applications. Medium ammonia latex strikes a balance, providing versatility in varied applications without the extremes of high or low ammonia options.</p></p>
