<p><p>The ultraviolet sensor market comprises three main types based on wavelength sensitivity: UVA sensors detect long-wave ultraviolet radiation, typically used in skincare and environmental monitoring. UVB sensors target medium-wave UV radiation, which is crucial for assessing sun exposure and its effects on skin cancer risks. UVC sensors measure short-wave UV radiation, often employed in sterilization and disinfection applications. Together, these sensors play a vital role in safety, health, and environmental monitoring, catering to various industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
