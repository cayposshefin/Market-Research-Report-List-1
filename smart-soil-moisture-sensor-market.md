<p><ul><li>Volumetric Soil Moisture Sensors</li><li>Soil Water Potential Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Soil Moisture Sensor market includes several types, primarily Volumetric Soil Moisture Sensors and Soil Water Potential Sensors. Volumetric Soil Moisture Sensors measure the actual volume of water content in the soil, providing precise moisture levels. In contrast, Soil Water Potential Sensors assess the energy status of water within the soil, indicating how readily plants can access moisture. These technologies enhance agricultural productivity by enabling efficient irrigation and promoting sustainable water management practices.</p></p>
