<p><ul><li>Manual Operation</li><li>Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The extension cord reels market comprises two main types: manual operation and automatic operation. Manual operation reels require users to manually wind and unwind the cord, providing control but demanding physical effort. These are typically more affordable and straightforward. In contrast, automatic reels feature spring mechanisms that retract the cord with a simple tug. This type offers convenience, reducing tangles and storage issues, making it popular for both residential and industrial applications where ease of use is essential.</p></p>
