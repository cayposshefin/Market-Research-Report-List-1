<p><strong>The Static Suppressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray Type</li><li>Electronic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The static suppressor market consists of two main types: spray type and electronic type. Spray type suppressors employ conductive sprays to neutralize static charges on surfaces, making them ideal for applications requiring quick and efficient static dissipation. In contrast, electronic type suppressors utilize electrical mechanisms, such as ion generators or active discharge systems, to eliminate static electricity, offering greater control and precision in managing static in sensitive environments. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and maintaining product integrity in various industries.</p></p>
