<p><p>The special transformers market is primarily divided into liquid-filled and dry-type transformers. Liquid-filled transformers utilize insulating oil to cool and protect the internal components, making them suitable for high-capacity applications and harsh environments. In contrast, dry-type transformers use air for cooling and are typically deployed in indoor settings where fire safety is a concern. Both types serve essential roles across various industries, including power generation, manufacturing, and renewable energy, offering distinct advantages based on application requirements.</p></p>
