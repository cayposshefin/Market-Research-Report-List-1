<p><p>The automotive plastic materials market encompasses various types that serve distinct functions in vehicle manufacturing. Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC) is used for its durability, and Polyamide (PA) offers thermal stability. Polycarbonate (PC) is favored for its clarity and toughness, and Polyvinyl Butyral (PVB) is prized for its adhesive properties in safety glass applications.</p></p>
