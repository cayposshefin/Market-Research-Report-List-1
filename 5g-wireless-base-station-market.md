<p><p>The 5G wireless base station market comprises various types that cater to different coverage and capacity needs. Macro base stations offer extensive coverage, typically covering several kilometers and serving urban and rural areas. Micro base stations provide enhanced coverage in smaller areas, ideal for urban environments. Pico base stations target even smaller hotspots, such as office buildings or shopping centers. Femto base stations are designed for residential use, enhancing indoor connectivity by connecting directly to broadband networks. Each type plays a crucial role in delivering 5G services.</p></p>
