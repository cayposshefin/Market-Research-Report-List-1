<p><p>The Cystic Fibrosis (CF) therapeutics market comprises several key types. Pancreatic enzyme supplements aid digestion by compensating for pancreatic insufficiency. Mucolytics work to thin mucus, promoting easier clearance from the lungs. Bronchodilators relax airway muscles, improving airflow and lung function. CFTR modulators target the underlying genetic defect, enhancing the function of the cystic fibrosis transmembrane conductance regulator protein. Together, these therapies address various aspects of CF, improving patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
