<p><strong>The Piezoelectric Speaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Piezo</li><li>Passive Piezo</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoelectric speaker market is divided into active and passive types. Active piezo speakers incorporate built-in amplifiers, allowing them to produce sound directly from an electrical signal, making them compact and efficient for applications like mobile devices and alarms. In contrast, passive piezo speakers require an external amplifier to generate sound, often offering better sound quality and higher output power, making them suitable for larger systems like audio equipment and public address systems. Both types leverage piezoelectric materials for sound generation, catering to diverse applications.</p></p>
