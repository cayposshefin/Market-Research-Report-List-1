<p><p>The cast waterproof protectors market encompasses three main types: plaster protection, bandage protection, and IV tube protection. Plaster protection is designed to safeguard traditional plaster casts from water exposure, ensuring they remain dry and intact. Bandage protection offers a waterproof barrier for patients with dressings or bandages, facilitating showering or bathing without compromising healing. IV tube protection focuses on shielding intravenous lines from moisture, preventing disruption of medication delivery while maintaining patient comfort and hygiene during water-related activities.</p></p>
