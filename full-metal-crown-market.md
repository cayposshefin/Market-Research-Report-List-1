<p><p>The Full Metal Crown market encompasses three primary types: Titanium, Base-Metal Alloy, and Noble Metal Alloy. Titanium crowns are prized for their strength and biocompatibility, making them suitable for dental applications. Base-metal alloys, typically less expensive, offer durability and resistance to corrosion, but may have aesthetic limitations. Noble metal alloys, containing precious metals like gold, provide excellent aesthetics and longevity but come at a higher cost. Each type serves different patient needs based on factors like budget, durability, and appearance.</p></p>
