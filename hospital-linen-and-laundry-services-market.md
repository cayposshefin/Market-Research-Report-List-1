<p><p>The Hospital Linen and Laundry Services market consists of two main types: rental services and customer-owned goods. Rental services involve hospitals outsourcing their linen and laundry needs to service providers, which supply clean, hygienic linens on a contractual basis. This helps hospitals manage costs and ensure compliance with hygiene standards. On the other hand, customer-owned goods involve hospitals using their own linens, which are cleaned in-house or sent to external laundries, providing more control over inventory and quality.</p></p>
