<p><ul><li>Magnetic Encoder Chips</li><li>Optical Encoder Chips</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The encoder chips market is primarily divided into magnetic and optical encoder chips. Magnetic encoder chips utilize magnetic fields to detect position and movement, offering robustness in harsh environments and high reliability. They are commonly used in industrial applications and robotics. In contrast, optical encoder chips use light and optical sensors to determine position, providing high precision and resolution. These are often found in consumer electronics, automotive, and automation systems where accuracy is critical. Both types cater to diverse market needs.</p></p>
