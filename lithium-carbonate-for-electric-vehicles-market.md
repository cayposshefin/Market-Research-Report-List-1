<p><ul><li>Salt Lake Extraction</li><li>Extraction of lithium ore</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lithium carbonate for electric vehicles is primarily sourced from two market types: salt lake extraction and lithium ore extraction. Salt lake extraction involves harvesting lithium-rich brine from underground reservoirs, primarily found in salt flats, where the brine is pumped to the surface and evaporated to concentrate lithium. In contrast, lithium ore extraction involves mining hard rock deposits, such as spodumene, where lithium is extracted through chemical processes. Both methods provide essential lithium carbonate used in EV batteries, driving growth in the electric vehicle market.</p></p>
