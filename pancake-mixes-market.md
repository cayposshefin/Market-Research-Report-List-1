<p><p>The pancake mix market is primarily divided into two types: dry pancake mixes and liquid pancake mixes. Dry pancake mixes are convenient, shelf-stable products that require the addition of wet ingredients like water or milk before cooking. They are popular for their long shelf life and ease of preparation. In contrast, liquid pancake mixes are ready-to-use solutions that eliminate the need for additional mixing, offering quick preparation and a consistent batter. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and preparation methods.</p></p>
