<p><ul><li>Analog Output Hall Sensor</li><li>Digital Output Hall Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Hall Effect Sensor IC market can be categorized into two main types: Analog Output Hall Sensors and Digital Output Hall Sensors. Analog Output Hall Sensors provide a continuous voltage signal proportional to the detected magnetic field strength, making them suitable for applications requiring precise measurements. In contrast, Digital Output Hall Sensors produce a binary signal, indicating the presence or absence of a magnetic field, ideal for applications like proximity sensing and switching. Each type serves distinct needs in various industries.</p></p>
