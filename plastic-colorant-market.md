<p><p>The plastic colorant market consists of three main types: inorganic pigments, organic pigments, and dyes. Inorganic pigments, derived from mineral sources, offer excellent stability and opacity, making them suitable for various applications. Organic pigments are synthesized from carbon-based compounds, providing vibrant colors and versatility, but may have lower thermal stability. Dyes, which dissolve in plastic matrices, impart bright colors but can be less lightfast than pigments. Each type serves distinct roles in enhancing plastic aesthetics and performance across industries.</p></p>
