<p><strong>The Voice over LTE (VoLTE) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voice Over IP Multimedia Subsystem (VoIMS)</li><li>Circuit Switched Fall Back (CSFB)</li><li>Dual Radio/Simultaneous Voice and LTE (SVLTE)</li><li>Voice Over LTE Via Generic Access Network (VOLGA)</li><li>Single Radio Voice Call Continuity (SRVCC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Voice over LTE (VoLTE) enables high-quality voice calls over LTE networks. Market types include Voice Over IP Multimedia Subsystem (VoIMS), which utilizes IMS architecture for voice services. Circuit Switched Fall Back (CSFB) falls back to 2G/3G for voice calls when LTE isn't available. Dual Radio enables simultaneous voice and LTE data. Voice Over LTE Via Generic Access Network (VOLGA) utilizes WLAN for voice calls. Single Radio Voice Call Continuity (SRVCC) ensures call continuity when switching between LTE and circuit-switched networks.</p></p>
