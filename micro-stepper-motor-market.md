<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Double Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro stepper motor market can be categorized into single stage and double stage types. Single stage micro stepper motors operate with a single winding configuration, providing precise control for simpler applications, such as small robotics and consumer electronics. In contrast, double stage micro stepper motors utilize two winding configurations to facilitate higher torque and enhanced positioning accuracy, making them suitable for complex systems like CNC machines and 3D printers. Both types cater to varying performance needs in automation and control applications.</p></p>
