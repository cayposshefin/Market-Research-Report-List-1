<p><p>The automotive heat treatment market encompasses various methods to enhance the mechanical properties of materials. Solution heat treatment involves heating materials to dissolve solute elements, followed by rapid cooling. Solution annealing is similar but focuses on relieving stresses. Water quenching rapidly cools materials in water to achieve desired hardness. Polymer or glycol quenching uses polymer or glycol solutions for controlled cooling, reducing cracking risks. Other methods may include gas quenching or cryogenic treatments, each tailored for specific automotive applications.</p></p>
