<p><p>The Microecological Modulator Market consists of three main types: probiotics, prebiotics, and synbiotics. Probiotics are live beneficial bacteria that support gut health and overall well-being. Prebiotics are non-digestible fibers that promote the growth of these beneficial bacteria in the gut. Synbiotics combine both probiotics and prebiotics, enhancing their effectiveness by providing both live microorganisms and the nutrients needed for their growth. Together, these products aim to improve digestive health and maintain a balanced gut microbiome.</p></p>
