<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi Layers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thick film ceramic PCBs are categorized into two main types: single layer and multi-layer. </p><p>Single layer PCBs consist of one thick film layer, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for less complex circuits. They are ideal for applications with lower density requirements. </p><p>In contrast, multi-layer PCBs incorporate multiple layers of ceramic substrates, enabling higher circuit density and supporting intricate designs. This type is suited for advanced electronic applications where greater functionality and interconnectivity are essential. Both types serve critical roles in various industries.</p></p>
