<p><p>The tactile and dimensional printing market encompasses various printing techniques that create textured and three-dimensional effects. Gravure printing utilizes engraved cylinders for high-quality images, ideal for long runs. Flexography employs flexible relief plates for printing on diverse substrates, suitable for packaging. Screen printing uses mesh to transfer ink, allowing for vibrant colors and thick applications, often used for textiles and promotional items. Inkjet printing offers versatility and precision, making it popular for short runs and customization on various surfaces.</p></p>
